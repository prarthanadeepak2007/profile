My Hobbies & Interests

Here’s a glimpse into the things I love doing in my free time. Each hobby helps me learn, relax, and express myself in a different way.

Coding & Building Projects

I enjoy coding because it feels like solving puzzles that can actually impact people. I mostly work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. Building small websites and automating boring tasks is my favorite way to spend a weekend.

Reading Books

Books are my escape and my classroom. I switch between fiction and non-fiction, but I have a soft spot for mystery novels and books on psychology. My current read is teaching me more about how habits are formed.

Dancing

Dancing helps me disconnect from screens and just feel the music. I’ve been learning hip-hop and a bit of contemporary for the last year. It’s challenging but the energy after a good session is unmatched.

Singing

I’m not a professional, but singing is pure therapy for me. I mostly sing pop and old Malayalam melodies. Recording covers and listening back helps me improve little by little.
